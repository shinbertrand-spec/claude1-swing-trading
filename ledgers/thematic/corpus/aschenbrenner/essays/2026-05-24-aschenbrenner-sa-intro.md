# Situational Awareness — Introduction

> Source: `raw/2026-05-24-aschenbrenner-sa-intro.md` (situational-awareness.ai)
> Ingested: 2026-05-24
> Author: [[leopold-aschenbrenner]]

## TL;DR

- ~A few hundred people, mostly in SF and AI labs, have genuine [[situational-awareness-thesis|situational awareness]] of what's coming — most of the world is dramatically underestimating pace + scale.
- Insiders are mobilizing trillions in compute + electricity for the [[agi]] race. "Every six months another zero is added to the boardroom plans" — $10B → $100B → trillion-dollar clusters.
- AGI by 2025/26 (college-graduate-level); [[superintelligence]] by end of decade. By 2027/28 expect "some form of government AGI project" — no startup can handle superintelligence.
- Prior "doomsayers" were derided but correctly predicted recent advances by trusting trend extrapolation; today's situationally-aware few may be "Szilard / Oppenheimer / Teller" analogues.
- Stakes are existential: superintelligence winner takes decisive economic + military advantage; failure modes include self-destruction and authoritarian takeover. "If we're lucky, we'll be in an all-out race with the CCP; if we're unlucky, an all-out war."

## Key claims

- **Exponential compute scaleup is observable now, in board-room plans, not speculation.** Cluster sizes scaling $10B → $100B → $1T in 1.5-2 year windows. Electricity procurement is the binding constraint.
- **The GPT-2-to-GPT-4 jump (4 years, preschooler → smart-high-schooler) is the empirical anchor** for extrapolating another human-intelligence-gap-sized jump 2023-2027. Argument detail follows in [[2026-05-24-aschenbrenner-sa-i-counting-the-ooms|Ch I]].
- **Categorical shift from tools to agents/coworkers:** "By 2025/26 these machines will outpace many college graduates. By the end of the decade, they will be smarter than you or I."
- **National-security mobilization is inevitable:** "By 27/28 we'll get some form of government AGI project" — leads into [[the-project]] thesis in [[2026-05-24-aschenbrenner-sa-iv-the-project|Ch IV]].
- **Mainstream consensus is wrong by orders of magnitude.** "Nvidia analysts still think 2024 might be close to the peak... Mainstream pundits are stuck on the willful blindness of 'it's just predicting the next word.'"
